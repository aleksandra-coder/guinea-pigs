About me

Guinea pigs are my beloved pets

image viaPeakpx

Hello, nice to meet you!

I am a guinea pig enthusiast. I love animals of any kind, but guinea pigs are my favourite. I own two guinea pigs, they are both girls of rosetta kind. Actually, the proper name of the breed is Abyssinian. I've got them for Christmas and it was the best present I could imagine. They live in a cage in my room. I am going to share some pictures and stories of my beloved pets on my blog.
